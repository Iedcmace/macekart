Hult Prize Oncampus Finals held at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering

28/02/2023

The latest edition of Hult Prize Oncampus Program was conducted at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam. The Hult Prize Oncampus phase is being held at over 121 countries around the world and 3,000 universities and campuses with this year's theme as Redefining Fashion: To make it more sustainable.The Oncampus Program had three industry experts Rajendra Prasad, Founder of ByHand, Geo Jose, Assistant district Industries officer, and Harsha Puthussery, Founder and MD of Iraaloom as the judges. The competition saw a high number of teams competing against each other wherin the team 'Eco Chic' emerged as the winners. The Oncampus winners would advance to compete in its Quarterfinal Phase and continue on the run for it's one-of-a-kind award in seed-captial of 1 million USD to change the world.
